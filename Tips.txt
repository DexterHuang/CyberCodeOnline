// Contains list of short tips used to be displayed in game
// one line one tip :)

Scrap your items to get tech scraps, then reverse engineer them to gain printing exp.
You can sell your items in the player market.
Your crafting ranks cannot exceed your level; don't waste time doing crafting research when you're capped.
HP gets set back to full after each battle, unless you're in a dungeon!
You always attack first; make good use of that advantage!
You can repair all your damaged equipped gear at the Weaponsmith.
Your inventory has a storage limit; keep an eye on it or you will lose loot.
Shields regenerate after you defeat an enemy in a dungeon.
In the chat terminal, you can press [Up Arrow] key to access the last message you send.
You can do reverse engineering at [JOB HUB].
You can skip waiting time by watching an ad in the Android app version.
You can skip/refresh random quests by watching ads in the Android app version.
"Trash" quality equipment is still useful for getting tech scraps and gaining printing exp.
No friends online to help with that dungeon quest? Try posting your Dungeon ID in the chat!
Craft painAway to be able to recover health between fights in dungeons.
In the chat terminal, type @ to mention another player.
Dungeons are a great source for AI Cores and Hash Processors.
Scavenge to find components for crafting ammo and medicine.
Printing always results in items the same level as your printing rank.
Beware of enemies with extra description such as tough, angry or shielded.
Secret keys are needed to access higher level stations; search dungeons for their fragments!
Detach upgrade modules when switching equipped gear to make available for the new gear.
Gear and upgrades "of expansion" increase your inventory size!
Some medicines such as painAwaySpray heal your teammates as well!
BTC in this game isn't redeemable, it's just in game currency. :'(
Don't forget to send a thank you to @DexterHuang in chat for making this game.
You can store excess upgrade modules in high-quality pocketed equiptment to save inventory space.
Dying a lot? Level up your printing rank! Keep this up with your player level!!!
Feeling faint? Stop playing for five minutes and get some food and water!
Remember the CyberCodeOnline mantra: Hit, hit, heal.
Running out of ammo? Craft some more through your invetory. (Under crafting > ammo)
Love the game?? Be sure to leave a review on the apple store/ google play store.
Not leveling as fast as you liked to? Go dungeon diving!! (It gives you 2.5x XP!)
Want to level a skill but havn't got the right resource? Check out [Job Hub] and pick up an odd job.
Speical weapons uses energy cell. Destructive weapons uses anti matter charges.
Its dangerous to go alone (in a challenge dungeon). 
Always be prepared with healing items and ammos. Have extra materials in case you need to craft more mid dungeon.
Got extra cache you dont have to lunge around anymore? Visit the bank today!
