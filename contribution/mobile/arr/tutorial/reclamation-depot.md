# Reclamation Depot

The **Reclamation Depot** be a community item donation system. Its main purpose be t' let scallywags get rid o' extra items they don't need while feedin' th' shared reward pool. Sometimes ye may also earn vouchers along th' way.

## Requirements

- There be a level requirement t' donate items.
- Redeemin' vouchers unlocks earlier than donatin'.
- Check th' game UI if ye aren't sure whether yer character can use it yet.

## How Donation Works

When ye donate items, they be removed from yer inventory and added t' th' shared reward pool.

Only certain items can be donated, and some o' them must be donated in fixed stack sizes. Th' game will show ye what can be selected.

Sometimes ye will receive a voucher fer donatin' items. Th' type o' voucher depends on th' item donated.
Ye can then use th' voucher t' redeem items from th' shared pool.

Donations be permanent and cannot be undone.

## Voucher Types

There be two voucher tiers in th' Reclamation Depot.

### Low Reclamation Voucher

Low vouchers usually come from more common or lower-value donation items.

### High Reclamation Voucher

High vouchers usually come from more valuable donation items.

## How Redemption Works

Ye can spend a voucher t' redeem a random reward from that voucher tier.

Rewards come from th' current shared pool, so what ye get depends on what scallywags have donated. Items with more stock in th' pool be more likely t' be redeemed. Th' item counts shown in th' depot be approximate and update from time t' time.
