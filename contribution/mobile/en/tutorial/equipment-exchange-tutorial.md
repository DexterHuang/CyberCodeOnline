## Equipment Exchange

Equipment Exchange lets you trade EPIC-tier equipment you no longer need for useful rewards. By submitting four eligible EPIC items you will receive a random payout of Epic Cache Fragments; the exact reward probabilities are displayed in the game's user interface and may change over time.

Equipment extraction does not require any buff or activation - you can exchange your unwanted EPIC equipment anytime! Only unlocked, non-backpack EPIC items that are currently in your inventory (not in the vault) can be selected.

## What Is Equipment Exchange?

Equipment Exchange lets players trade unwanted EPIC-tier equipment for useful rewards. By exchanging four eligible EPIC items you receive a random payout of Epic Cache Fragments; the exact probabilities are shown in the game UI and may change over time.

## Benefits of Equipment Exchange

- Excellent for inventory management - convert multiple EPIC items into compact fragments
- Clears unused EPIC items from your inventory
- Generates Epic Cache Fragments that can be fused into guaranteed EPIC equipment

## How to Access Equipment Exchange

Access the Equipment Exchange feature through the Equipment Exchange NPC. This feature is available to all players.

## Exchanging Epic Equipment for Epic Cache Fragments

1. Open the Equipment Exchange page.
2. Select four unlocked, non-backpack EPIC items from your inventory.
3. Confirm the exchange to receive your reward - you will get between around 4 Epic Cache Fragments based on random chance. specific chance please check the game UI.

**No buff or activation required** - you can extract equipment anytime!

## Fusing Epic Cache Fragments into Epic Equipment Caches

1. Collect eight Epic Cache Fragments (obtained from equipment extraction).
2. Activate the `Epic Fragment Fusion Access` buff (available in shop or from global buffs).
3. Navigate to the Fragment Fusion page.
4. Confirm to fuse fragments into one Epic Equipment Cache containing guaranteed EPIC gear.

**Note**: Fragment fusion requires the `Epic Fragment Fusion Access` buff to be active. The buff has a cooldown - you can only fuse once per buff activation.

## Eligibility Requirements for Epic Equipment Exchange

- Item must be EPIC tier.
- Item cannot be a Backpack.
- Item must be unlocked.
- Item must reside in your inventory (not the vault).
