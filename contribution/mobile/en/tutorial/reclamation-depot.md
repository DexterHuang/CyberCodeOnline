# Reclamation Depot

The **Reclamation Depot** is a community item donation system. Its main purpose is to let players get rid of extra items they do not need while feeding the community reward pool. Sometimes you may also earn vouchers along the way.

## Requirements

- There is a level requirement to donate items.
- Redeeming vouchers unlocks earlier than donating.
- Check the game UI if you are unsure whether your character can use it yet.

## How Donation Works

When you donate items, they are removed from your inventory and added to the shared reward pool.

Only certain items can be donated, and some of them must be donated in fixed stack sizes. The game will show you what can be selected.

Sometimes you will receive a voucher for donating items. The type of voucher depends on the item donated.
You then can use the voucher to redeem items from the shared pool.

Donations are permanent and cannot be undone.

## Voucher Types

There are two voucher tiers in the Reclamation Depot.

### Low Reclamation Voucher

Low vouchers usually come from more common or lower-value donation items.

### High Reclamation Voucher

High vouchers usually come from more valuable donation items.

## How Redemption Works

You can spend a voucher to redeem a random reward from that voucher tier.

Rewards come from the current shared pool, so what you get depends on what players have donated. Items with more stock in the pool are more likely to be redeemed. The item counts shown in the depot are approximate and update periodically.
